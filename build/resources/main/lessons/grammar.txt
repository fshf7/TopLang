Grammar in Use
What tenses do you know in the English language?

There are 12 tenses in the Active Voice in the English language. Four of them are present tenses. They are: Present Simple, Present Continuous, Present Perfect and Present Perfect Continuous. They all have their special features and different rules of formation. Today we are going to revise these tenses and discuss them in details.
