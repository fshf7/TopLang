The recycling loop
Recycling? Recycling. Recycling!

We often hear the word recycling, but what does it actually mean? Basically recycling is some waste turned into some new product. Almost any waste can be recycled and turned into something new, consisting of the reused material. It’s a great idea, isn’t it?
